# Awesome Auto-GPT

Auto-GPT is a pioneering open-source project designed to demonstrate the power of the GPT-4 language model. Harnessing the impressive abilities of GPT-4, this application seamlessly links together Large Language Model (LLM) "thoughts" to independently accomplish any objective you designate. As one of the premier instances of GPT-4 operating in a completely autonomous manner, Auto-GPT is redefining the frontier of AI capabilities.

## Research Papers

- [Auto-GPT for Online Decision Making: Benchmarks and Additional Opinions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.02224)
Abstract: This paper presents a comprehensive benchmark study of Auto-GPT styled agents in decision-making tasks, comparing the performance of popular LLMs such as GPT-4, GPT-3.5, Claude, and Vicuna. Additionally, an easy and effective method called the Additional Opinions algorithm is introduced to enhance the Auto-GPT scheme.
- [Lottery and Sprint: Generate a Board Game with Design Sprint and Auto GPT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2307.00348)
Abstract: This paper explores the use of the Auto GPT system alongside Design Sprint methodology to facilitate the creation of diverse board games. The subsequent optimization process and user study provide insights into the playability and enjoyment of the generated games.
- [AutoML-GPT: Automatic Machine Learning with GPT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.02499)
Abstract: AutoML-GPT proposes an approach to automate the training pipeline by developing task-oriented prompts and utilizing GPT as the bridge to diverse AI models. It dynamically composes prompt paragraphs based on user requests from model and data cards, enabling automated data processing, hyperparameter tuning, and training of AI models.
- [AD-AutoGPT: An Autonomous GPT for Alzheimer's Disease](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.10095)
Abstract: This pioneering study introduces AD-AutoGPT, an autonomous application based on the GPT-4 large language model, for conducting data collection, processing, and analysis of complex health narratives related to Alzheimer's Disease. It showcases the transformative potential of AI in enhancing our understanding of public discourse on health issues.
- [From GPT to AutoGPT: A Brief Attention in NLP Processing using DL](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/370107237_From_GPT_to_AutoGPT_a_Brief_Attention_in_NLP_Processing_using_DL)
Abstract: This paper provides an overview of the evolution of Generative Pre-Training (GPT) models, highlighting the advancements from GPT-1 to the latest AutoGPT model. It discusses the applications of GPT models in Natural Language Processing and their implications in Deep Learning-based language processing.
- [arXiv:2304.09842v2 [cs.CL] 24 May 2023](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2304.09842)
Abstract: This research paper showcases the performance of Chameleon with GPT-4 on TabMWP, a mathematical reasoning benchmark with tabular contexts. Examples demonstrate the abilities and potential of the model in solving complex mathematical problems.
- [arXiVeri: Automatic Table Verification with GPT](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.07968)
Abstract: This paper addresses the challenge of accurate transcription of numerical data in scientific documents through the novel task of automatic table verification (AutoTV). It proposes a benchmark called arXiVeri, comprising tabular data from open-access academic papers on arXiv, and introduces metrics to evaluate the performance of a table verifier using modern large language models (LLMs).
- [The Potential Breakthroughs with ChatGPT in Parasitology](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC10423911/)
Abstract: This article discusses ChatGPT, an AI model based on the GPT architecture developed by OpenAI, and explores its potential applications in the field of Parasitology.
